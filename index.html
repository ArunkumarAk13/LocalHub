How It Works

📤 Post Your Need

Users post their needs under categories like mobiles, electronics, furniture, or any item they are searching for. This step makes it easy for others to know what's in demand.

🔔 Notify Sellers

Shopkeepers or individual sellers subscribe to specific categories by clicking the "Notify" button. This ensures they only get notified for relevant posts.

💬 Direct Contact

Once notified, sellers can directly reach out to the buyer using in-app chat or contact info. This encourages open communication and better negotiation.

🔒 Trust & Safety

Every profile is public with ratings and reviews, so users know who they're dealing with. High-rated users earn a "Highly Trusted" badge.

📦 Sell Instantly

Sellers can respond to real demand immediately, even without a full inventory. It enables anyone to sell extra or used items efficiently.

📲 Download & Start

Just download the app, create a profile, and start buying or selling instantly. No paperwork or complicated setup needed — start in seconds!
